Methodology & Assumptions

            
        

        

            

                
1) Theoretical Energy Estimation

                

                    Given your selected device type (Desktop / Laptop / Server),
                    whether a GPU is used, and the target model‚Äôs
                    parameter count, we run an internal regression model
                    to estimate the theoretical energy consumption per query
                    in kilowatt-hours (kWh).
                

            

            

                
2) Local Grid Mix & Carbon Intensity

                

                    From your location (auto-detected or manually set),
                    we fetch the current electricity mix using an Electricity Maps‚Äìlike API. We then multiply
                    the energy (kWh) by the location‚Äôs carbon intensity
                    (gCO‚ÇÇe/kWh) to get total emissions in grams CO‚ÇÇe.
                

            

            

                
3) Display Units

                
    
                    
  • Personal Use: Energy displayed in Wh (kWh √ó 1000), CO‚ÇÇ in grams.
                    
    • 
                    
  • Enterprise: Energy in kWh,
                        CO‚ÇÇ in kg.
                    
    • 
                

            

            

                
4) Equivalents & Factors

                
We convert results into simple everyday equivalents using the constants below:

                
    
                    
  • üì± Phone charge: 15 Wh per full
                        charge.
                    
    • 
                    
  • üí° LED hours: 10 W LED ‚Üí hours = Wh / 10.
                    
    • 
                    
  • üöó Car km: 120 g CO‚ÇÇe per km
                        (avg. small car).
                    
    • 
                

                

                    For Personal Use, car km is computed via CO‚ÇÇ:
                    km = carbon_g / 120.
                

            

            

                
5) Notes & Caveats

                
    
                    
  • Estimates depend on model size, device, and GPU usage; actual energy may vary with runtime,
                        batch size, precision, and system load.
                    
    • 
                    
  • Carbon intensity changes over time; figures reflect the current/selected mix at query time.
    • 
                    
  • Equivalents are rounded for readability and intended for order-of-magnitude understanding.
